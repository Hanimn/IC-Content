# Hint 1

The page says "Nothing to see here" — and on screen, that's true.

But a webpage isn't just what you see. Your browser received a full text file to build this page. What you're looking at is the *rendered* version. The original is still there.

What's the difference between what a browser *shows* you and what it actually *received*?
