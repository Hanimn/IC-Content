# Hint 2

Open DevTools → **Elements** panel (F12).

Press **Ctrl+F** (or Cmd+F on Mac) to search within the DOM. Type:

```
type="hidden"
```

You'll find two hidden inputs inside the form. They have `name` and `value` attributes — these are what get submitted when you click the button.

**Double-click the `value` attribute** of the price field to edit it inline. Change it to `0`. Press Enter. Then submit the form and see what happens.

After that, look at the second hidden field.
