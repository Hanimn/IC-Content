# Hint 1

The Helix Commerce checkout page shows a price of £49.99. But that number on screen is just a display label — it's not what the form actually submits.

When you click "Complete Purchase," the browser collects every input in the form and sends it to the server. That includes inputs you can't see by looking at the page.

Think about what Module 02 taught you about looking beyond what's visible. The Inspector shows you everything in the DOM — not just what renders on screen.
