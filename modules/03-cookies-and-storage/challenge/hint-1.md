# Hint 1

This page shows different content depending on your membership tier. But your tier isn't stored on a server somewhere — it's stored right here, in your browser.

Think about what you've learned in the previous modules. You've used the Application tab before. What kind of browser storage might a site use to remember who you are between page loads?

If the page trusts whatever your browser tells it about your tier — what happens when you change what your browser says?
