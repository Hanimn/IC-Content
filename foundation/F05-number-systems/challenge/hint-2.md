# Hint 2

**Panel 1**: 42 = 32 + 8 + 2. Which bit positions are those? (32 is the 6th bit, 8 is the 4th, 2 is the 2nd, counting from the right starting at 1.)

**Panel 2**: `10110100` split into nibbles: `1011` and `0100`. From the table: `1011` = B, `0100` = 4. The answer is two characters.

**Panel 3**: The first four bytes are `46 4C 41 47`. These are the hex codes for `F`, `L`, `A`, `G`. Does that look familiar? Keep going for the rest — the characters inside the braces spell the flag value.
