# Hint 1

This is a guided tour, not a hidden-flag hunt. There is nothing concealed in the page source. Every flag here is earned by walking through the six zones and answering one question each.

A few nudges:

- Zone 1 and Zone 3 are about reading **paths**. Read them top-down, slash by slash.
- Zone 2 is the only zone where you have to **do something with the keyboard** instead of clicking a choice. Re-read the lesson section on keyboard shortcuts if you forgot which one to use.
- Zone 4 wants you to remember that "the internet" and "a browser" are not the same thing.
- Zone 5 wants you to remember that a server is not a magic device.
- Zone 6 has only one ethically defensible answer.

If a zone keeps rejecting your answer, close the modal, go back to the lesson section that covers it, then come back. The lesson and the challenge mirror each other one-for-one.
