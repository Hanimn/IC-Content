# Hint 1

You have a ciphertext and a wheel. The wheel can show you every possible shift — there are only 25 of them.

Try moving the slider slowly from left to right. Watch the **Decoded Output** box as you move it. At most positions, the output looks like random gibberish. But at one specific position, the jumbled letters will suddenly snap into recognizable English words.

When the decoded text starts looking like a real sentence, you've found the key. Trust your eyes — you'll know it when you see it.
