# Hint 2

Still stuck? Here's the chain, with one piece missing from each step:

1. Your browser needs a _____ before it can connect. (It's not the web page — that comes later.)
2. When the DNS resolver doesn't know the answer, it _____ another server.
3. The root nameserver doesn't give the IP directly — it gives a _____ to the right specialist.
4. The .com nameserver also gives a referral — to the _____ nameserver for that specific domain.
5. The authoritative nameserver finally gives the _____ back.

The pattern: each step is a referral, getting more specific, until the final step gives the actual IP.
